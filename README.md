# Campushub
## Overview
Campushub is a mobile application designed to provide students with a centralized platform for communication and campus services. It helps students access important announcements and trending topics as well as discovering campus events. Students can also use the platform to buy and sell items, report lost property and post found items to help reunite ownwers with their belongings.

## Problem statement 
University students rely on vairous platforms to access campus information and services.University students often rely on multiple platforms such as email, WhatsApp groups, and physical notice boards to receive important updates from student leaders and the university administration.This fragmented communication causes students to miss important announcements, events, and opportunities.. This is where campushub comes in. It is meant to centralize all communication services into one.

## Objectives
- Make communicaton easier.
- Help studnts recover lost items faster.
- Create a marketplace fo students.
- Promote campus events and increase student participation.
- Improve communication between students and campus leadership.
